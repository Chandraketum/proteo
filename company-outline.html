From Science to Solutions – Services That Matter

Driving Scientific Research – Made in India, Made for the World

At Proteogenixx, we believe in creating research solutions that are truly Made in India. With a commitment to self-manufacturing, we design, develop, and produce a wide range of molecular biology and protein research products that cater to both academic and industrial research needs. By manufacturing in-house, we ensure quality control, cost-effectiveness, and innovation at every step. Our “Made in India” vision is not just about products—it’s about empowering Indian researchers with globally competitive tools that accelerate discoveries.

Innovating at the BSC BioNEST Bio-Incubator (BBB), RCB

We are proud to be incubated at the BSC BioNEST Bio-Incubator (BBB), Regional Centre for Biotechnology (RCB), Faridabad. This state-of-the-art ecosystem nurtures biotech startups and provides cutting-edge infrastructure for product development. At BBB-RCB, Proteogenixx is actively engaged in research projects, collaborative innovation, and translational science. This platform enables us to transform scientific ideas into market-ready solutions that can address global challenges in biotechnology and life sciences.

Industrial Training – From Learning to Application

Proteogenixx is dedicated to shaping the next generation of researchers by offering industrial training programs that go beyond theory. Our hands-on modules cover molecular biology techniques, protein research tools, and product application training. Students, scholars, and industry professionals gain practical exposure to the latest laboratory practices, preparing them for careers in academia, research, and biotech industries. With expert mentorship and real-time learning, our training acts as a bridge between classroom knowledge and industrial requirements.

Specialized Services – Protein Analysis & Estimation

Proteogenixx offers comprehensive protein analysis and estimation services to support laboratories and industries in achieving precise and reliable data. Our services include quantitative protein assays, characterization, and advanced detection methods. By using standardized protocols and high-quality reagents, we provide accurate results that are crucial for downstream research applications such as structural studies, enzyme activity assays, and therapeutic development. We help researchers save time and resources while ensuring reproducibility and consistency in results.

Proteogenixx, are committed to redefining the frontiers of life sciences by delivering precisely engineered, high-quality research solutions that underpin the very fabric of modern biology.
