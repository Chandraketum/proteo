Geneaid Established in 2002, has become a trusted global manufacturer of high-quality DNA/RNA purification technologies. With a comprehensive portfolio, Geneaid supports molecular biology, biotechnology, diagnostics, and clinical research worldwide. Its products cover plasmid DNA preparation kits, genomic DNA and RNA extraction kits, viral nucleic acid kits, gel extraction kits, PCR cleanup kits, cloning systems, DNA markers, enzymes, and protein research tools — providing complete solutions for nucleic acid and protein analysis.

To match the diverse workflows of modern laboratories, Geneaid employs three core methodologies:

  • Spin Column Technology – known for its simplicity, reproducibility, and speed, making it the preferred choice for routine DNA and RNA purification.
  • Bead–Based Extraction – offers high purity, scalability, and seamless integration with automated platforms, ideal for labs seeking efficiency and flexibility.
  • High-Throughput 96-Well Systems – designed for parallel processing and large sample volumes, delivering consistency and time-saving benefits for high-demand research and clinical testing.

By combining vigorousinteraction with innovative formats, Geneaid enables researchers to choose the method best suited to their application — whether manual, semi-automated, or fully automated — ensuring reliable results from single experiments to industrial-scale projects.
