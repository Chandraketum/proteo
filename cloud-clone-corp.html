Proteogenixx proudly serves as the authorized distributor of Cloud-Clone technologies, delivering their world-class research tools and innovative solutions to scientific institutions and laboratories across India.

Cloud-Clone Corp. (CCC, USA), headquartered in Houston, is a global biotechnology company specializing in molecular biology and immunology. As the trademark owner of “Cloud-Clone”, CCC focuses on producing high-quality assay reagents and providing animal experiment services, with 75% of its customers based in the USA and Europe.

Cloud-Clone Corp. offers a complete suite of research tools to accelerate life science discoveries.

Extensive Protein Library (11,000+)

A wide collection of recombinant and natural proteins, including cytokines, enzymes, growth factors, receptors, and other biologically active molecules for research and diagnostics.

Comprehensive Antibody (19,000+)

A comprehensive range of monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies tailored for immunology, oncology, neuroscience, infectious diseases, and other key research areas.

Diverse Immunoassay Solutions (7,000+)

An extensive portfolio of ELISA and other immunoassay kits, enabling accurate detection and quantification of proteins, peptides, and biomarkers across diverse applications.

Its advanced production platforms span protein synthesis and purification, antibody development, and assay kit manufacturing (ELISA & CLIA), ensuring reliable solutions for researchers worldwide.

With its advanced platforms, comprehensive product catalog, and global reach, Cloud-Clone Corp. continues to support scientists, researchers, and clinicians in driving progress across molecular biology, immunology, and life science research.
